Regulatory Framework

Compliance isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Designed from day one for Kenya's regulatory environment. Every module maps to a specific CMA, CBK, or ODPC requirement.

CMA
Capital Markets Authority
VaTViT is being designed for CMA Regulatory Sandbox entry. Our system boundary design ensures we operate as a Financial Operating System — not a broker, and not a fund manager.
✓ Sandbox application in preparation
CBK
Central Bank of Kenya
AML transaction monitoring, sanctions screening against CBK watchlists, and 7-year document retention in full compliance with CBK anti-money laundering guidelines.
✓ AML engine operational at launch
ODPC
Data Protection Act
All personal data of Kenyan users stored in-country. Data Processing Agreements in place with all third-party processors. Right-to-erasure supported natively in the platform.
✓ Nairobi-region data residency
